Wisconsin Geography Club

Here you will find information and updates regarding the Wisconsin Geography Club. The club focuses on helping undergraduate students to build connections with their peers, faculty, and in the many professional fields related to geography. Meetings are biweekly on Tuesdays at 5:30 pm during the semester with occasional special events on the opposite weeks.
